Blitz.gg Valorant Lineups: Case Study

From concept to 1,000+ videos - delivering competitive insights to millions.

Role & Context

As a Product Manager at Blitz.gg, I led strategy and agile delivery for a platform with over 2.5M monthly users. Our goal was to enhance the Valorant player experience by providing highly curated lineup videos directly within the desktop app.

Problem

Players struggled to find reliable, in-context lineup guides without tabbing out to YouTube or Reddit. The lack of embedded, high-quality educational content created friction for both casual and competitive users.

Solution

We built a new feature that delivered curated Valorant lineup videos, categorized by agent, map, and utility type, directly within the Blitz desktop client. I collaborated with our UX team to design a branded overlay system, which included the Blitz logo, animated transitions, and unique visual formats for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd-person POVs.

Process

  • Directed the full product lifecycle for 15+ features, focusing on this video overlay and playback experience.
  • Led backlog refinement across 3 cross-functional teams (product, content, engineering) to prioritize impactful releases.
  • Used agile ceremonies and user feedback loops to iterate rapidly and ship updates weekly.
  • Implemented streamlined review cycles and workflows that cut content production time by 30%.

Results

  • Scaled the lineup library from 0 to over 1,000 videos across agents and maps.
  • Increased platform engagement by 40% among tactical players using lineup features.
  • Improved sprint throughput by 25% and reduced triage time by 40%.
  • Achieved 95% on-time release success across lineup-related updates.

Reflection

This project reinforced how design, iteration, and velocity are interconnected. By creating strong alignment between product, UX, and content teams, we delivered a feature that truly empowered competitive players. It taught me the value of MVP scoping, cross-team feedback loops, and keeping visual polish aligned with brand identity.
