Senior PMGaming & Social

Overview

A tool that automatically captured and organized headshot kills for player review and esports content production.

Problem

Competitive players and content teams spent hours compiling highlight reels for social and broadcast use.

Role

Role: Senior Product Manager

Scoped MVP, aligned with esports teams for pilot usage, and coordinated real-time capture with engineering.

Core Skills Demonstrated

Overlay tools Real-time events API integration Esports workflows MVP pilots

Solution

  • Lightweight client overlay detecting, flagging, and clipping headshots.
  • Searchable tagging system for fast retrieval by map, agent, and weapon.
  • One-click export to social-ready aspect ratios and codecs.

Impact

  • Adopted by 3 esports teams during testing.
  • Reduced highlight compilation time from hours to minutes.
  • Sunset due to third-party API policy changes.
